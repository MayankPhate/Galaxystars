Galaxy

A galaxy is a huge collection of gas, dust, and billions of stars and their solar systems,
all held together by gravity. We live on a planet called Earth that is part of our solar system.

The Planets

There are eight recognized planets in our solar system. They are, in order from the Sun:

  1. Mercury
  2. Venus
  3. Earth
  4. Mars
  5. Jupiter
  6. Saturn
  7. Uranus
  8. Neptune
